About Me

I was born and raised in Tullahoma, TN. I moved to New York in 2023 to pursue my passion for photography and social change at Pratt Institute. I have been fascinated by art since long before I could ever define or properly grasp the concept. I was given my first camera at the age of four, and yet, my understanding of what constitutes AS art continues to evolve even today. The one constant of art is that it is a universal way of communication, and that mere fact inspires me to continue making work - prompting conversation and setting out to make change.
